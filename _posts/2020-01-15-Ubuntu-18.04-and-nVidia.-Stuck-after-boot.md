If your computer gets stuck after boot or your live USB doesn't even boot, and you have an nVidia graphic card, you are probably facing this issue.
The missing of nVidia driver causes this freeze.
In my case, the solution was this:

1. Disable Fast Boot and Secure Boot (or Secure loader) if active.
2. Turn on the pc and press `shift` or `esc` during boot to show the GRUB menu.
3. Press `e` and edit the line starting with `linux` adding `nouveau.modeset=0` or `nomodeset`.
4. Press `F10` to boot.

After these steps, if everything works, just install nvidia proprietary drivers and reboot again.
